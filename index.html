Good to know

Frequently asked questions.

What do you need to start an estimate? +
Your drawings (PDF or CAD), any specifications, and a note on the scope and trades you want priced. The more complete the set, the tighter the estimate.
How fast is turnaround? +
Most single-trade takeoffs are returned in 24–48 hours. Full multi-division BOQs depend on project size — we confirm a firm date with your free quote.
What format do I receive? +
A structured Excel workbook with quantities, a summary sheet, and a priced BOQ, plus a PDF copy. We can match your in-house template on request.
Which regions do you serve? +
Contractors worldwide — with deep experience in the USA, Canada, UK, Australia, Saudi Arabia and the UAE. Estimates are priced to your local market.
Is my project confidential? +
Always. Your drawings and pricing are never shared, and we’ll sign an NDA on request before any files are exchanged.
