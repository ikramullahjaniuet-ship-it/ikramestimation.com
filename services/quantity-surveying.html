Quantity Surveying Services.

BOQ preparation, valuations, variations and cost control — full QS support across the project lifecycle.

Overview

Cost control from tender to final account.

Beyond the takeoff, we provide full quantity surveying support: preparing bills of quantities, valuing work in progress, pricing variations and keeping a tight grip on cost as the project moves.

Whether you need a one-off BOQ or ongoing commercial support, the work is delivered with the rigour expected on Aramco-standard and international projects.

What we cover

  • Bill of Quantities (BOQ) preparation to standard methods.
  • Interim valuations and payment applications.
  • Variation and change-order pricing.
  • Cost reporting and budget tracking.
  • Final account preparation and agreement.

What you receive

  • Detailed quantity takeoff in structured Excel.
  • Priced BOQ with labour, material and markup breakdown.
  • A clear summary sheet, ready to drop into your bid.
  • One round of revisions included.
